White Water Rafting Company

To create unforgettable river adventures that connect people with nature, challenge their limits, and inspire lifelong memories.

History

In 1940, a group of adventurers launched the first commercial white water rafting trip down Wyoming’s Snake River using military-style inflatable rafts.

Their success sparked the founding of one of America’s earliest rafting companies,blending thrill-seeking with guided outdoor exploration.
